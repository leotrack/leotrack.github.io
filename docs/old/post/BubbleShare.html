图片分享新工具—BubbleShare


BubbleShare是一个新的图片发布（Or相册）平台，目前还处在测试（Beta）期，从FAQ里看到：
  • How long will my Album be available?
    Your photos are stored in low resolution for 365 days. At the end of the year you can renew your album for another free year at no cost.
  • 图片能存放1年
  • Are there other ways to upload?
    Yes, there are many ways to upload your photos to create an album:

    Internet Explorer - Click 'Try the Enhanced Uploader' and you will be provided with instructions on how to install and use our ActiveX uploader. This tool allows you to quickly drag and drop files right into the BubbleShare website.


    Firefox/Opera/Mozilla etc. - Click 'Try the Enhanced Uploader' and you can use our Flash based uploader. This uploader allows you to easily select more than one file at a time. When you click 'Add Photos' select multiple photos by holding down the CTRL key as you click on photo files. Once you have added all of your photos, click 'Create Album'.

    Macintosh iPhoto - Download the iPhoto plugin and you can create an album right from iPhoto.

  • 可以有多种上传方式，而且最好玩的是可以给图片加上声音说明哦（Audio Caption）！
  • 支持相册RSS和评论RSS功能。
